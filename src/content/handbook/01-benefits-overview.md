# Membership Benefits – Overview

This chapter provides a high-level overview of all membership benefits available through Friends of UCL Computer Science. Each benefit is listed with a short description outlining its purpose and value to partner organisations.

---

## IXN Undergraduate or Graduate Project Collaboration (B01)

Partner with a student team to explore a real research or innovation challenge. This provides early access to emerging talent while advancing ideas that benefit your organisation. Projects can support R&D pipelines, rapid prototyping, and feasibility exploration.

---

## Brand Visibility on the UCL Computer Science Website (B02)

Showcase your organisation as an innovation partner recognised by one of the UK’s leading Computer Science departments. This visibility strengthens your employer brand and signals commitment to research and talent development.

---

## Employer Engagement Events and Career Activities (B03)

Engage directly with students through career fairs, pop-up stands, and themed employer events. These activities support talent attraction and allow partners to raise awareness of opportunities within their organisation.

---

## Curated Short Courses for Workforce Development (B04)

Access a curated selection of UCL short courses designed to support upskilling, leadership development, and innovation capability building within partner organisations.

---

## Executive Education Taster Session (B05)

Participate in a tailored executive education taster session that showcases UCL Computer Science expertise and helps partners explore future professional development opportunities.

---

## Recruiter in Residence Scheme (B06)

Embed recruiters within the department for a defined period to engage with students, offer guidance, and promote career pathways.

---

## Innovation Workshops and Hackathons (B07)

Collaborate on themed innovation workshops or hackathons that bring together students, researchers, and industry partners to explore emerging challenges and technologies.

---

## Networking and Community Events (B08)

Access invitation-only networking events that connect partners with academics, students, and the wider innovation ecosystem.

---

## Research Translation and Knowledge Exchange Support (B09)

Receive support to explore research translation pathways, knowledge exchange opportunities, and collaborative funding bids.

---

## Strategic Partnership Development Support (B10)

Work with the Strategic Alliances Team to scope and develop deeper, longer-term strategic partnerships with UCL Computer Science.
